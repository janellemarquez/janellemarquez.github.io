BIOGRAPHY 

(continuation)

In 2014, I got an app called Stopmotion Studio Pro that allowed me to take pictures and put it all together to make a still life animation. I used it very often so I made a YouTube Channel to share these videos with other people. The more I practiced, the better my skills were. When it was time for me to apply for a magnet in the middle school i wanted to go to, I decided to enroll for Technology Production. I am very thankful I made this decision because I have learned many new things that make me better everyday.

In Technology Production, I have improved my editing skills. This is very useful for me because I could use this in my videos! In the 2 years that i have been here, I have already learned the basics of Photoshop and a variety of other programs. In 2016, I entered a competition for the youth fair about making a brochure about computer engineering. I used what I had learned and ended up getting 1st place! Mr. Pate is my Technology teacher and he has helped me get better in so many ways.

As stated in the first paragraph, I had also started a YouTube Channel. My videos were of poor quality and weren't very entertaining. Now, with all of these techniques I have developed, I have been very successful in my channel and have gained over 600 subscribers. The quality and smoothness of the motions have also improved! This is all thanks to a certain class that I joined and from practicing constantly.
